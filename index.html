Cosmic Combat

Cosmic Combat is a four-player party brawler where players choose their alien champion and compete for their place in the next pantheon of Galactic Gladiators

As narrative designer, my roles included fleshing out the game's backstory and lore, as well as developing the alien races and their champions.

Raccoon Rummage

Raccoon Rummage was developed for the 2022 SGDA Fall Game Jam in 40 hours. In it, players control a raccoon sifting for treasure in a terrifying human's garbage.

As narrative/game designer, I had the enviable task of coming up with mechanics that let players really feel like a raccoon. I also procured and edited all sound effects found in-game.

About

I'm proud to have converted games, my longtime hyperfixation, into an outlet for creative expression.
